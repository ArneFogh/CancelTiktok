Why you should delete Tiktok

Hackers have found a way to send a direct message to a user on Tiktok, and when clicked on, they now have access to all of their account. Which gives hackers and cybercriminals the ability to spread propaganda and manipulate users feeds, plant unsolicited content that could be misleading or disturbing, especially for younger people using Tiktok.
