Who are we?

The Tiktok aren't real movement have been around since the release of Tiktok in the US in September 2017.
Once a preventative cause, our initial goal was to stop the Chinese government entering our digital lives.
In these uncertain times it is important to know that the president of the United States of America, Donald Trump once said he would ban the Chinese owned app in the US. This was unsuccessful, and it is now thriving more than ever before. With over 1 billion active users and almost 40% of all US citizens with an internet connect use Tiktok on a monthly basis.
